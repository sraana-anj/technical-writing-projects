# **NoteNinja Quick Start Guide**

*Written by Raana sk.*

---

## **1\. Introduction**

NoteNinja is a simple note-taking web app designed for speed and organization. This guide will walk you through signing up, creating your first note, and syncing across devices.

By the end, you’ll have your own workspace ready for jotting down ideas, tasks, and to-dos.

---

## **2\. System Requirements**

Before starting, make sure you have:

* A stable internet connection

* A browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge)

* A valid email address

*(Tip: Works best on desktop for first-time setup.)*

---

## **3\. Sign Up for NoteNinja**

1. Visit **www.noteninja.io/signup**

2. Enter your name, email, and password.

3. Click **Create Account.**

   

---

## **4\. Create Your First Note**

1. Log in to your account.

2. Click **New Note** in the top toolbar.

3. Enter a title and start typing your note.

4. Click **Save.**

**Example:**

Title: Meeting Notes  
 Content: Discussed new features for Q4 release.

---

## **5\. Organize Your Notes**

1. Select **Folders** from the sidebar.

2. Click **\+ New Folder** and name it “Work Notes.”

3. Drag your note into the folder.

*(Insert screenshot: folder view showing one note moved inside “Work Notes.”)*

---

## **6\. Sync Across Devices**

1. Go to **Settings → Sync.**

2. Enable **Cloud Sync.**

3. Your notes are now accessible on any device.

---

## **7\. Shortcuts (Optional)**

| Action | Shortcut |
| ----- | ----- |
| Create new note | Ctrl \+ N |
| Save note | Ctrl \+ S |
| Search notes | Ctrl \+ F |
| Delete note | Del key |

---

## **8\. Summary**

You’ve created and synced your first note with NoteNinja.  
 From here, you can explore:

* Sharing notes with teammates

* Tagging and searching notes

* Exporting notes as PDFs
